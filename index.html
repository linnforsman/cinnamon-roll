Cinnamon roll is a sweet roll commonly served in Northen Europe and North America. In Sweden it is called kanelbulle, in Denmark it is known as kanelsnegl, in Norway it is known as Kanelbolle, Skillingsboller or Kanelsnurr, and in Finland it is known as korvapuusti.
